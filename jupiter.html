Jupiter: The Giant Planet

The largest planet with fierce storms and massive gravity.

A Gas Giant

Jupiter is made mostly of hydrogen and helium. It has no solid surface but a thick atmosphere with swirling storms.

What Makes Jupiter Unique?

Jupiter's Great Red Spot is a storm that has been raging for centuries. It has the strongest magnetic field of all planets.

Great Red Spot

A massive storm bigger than Earth, active for over 350 years.

79 Moons

Ganymede, the largest moon, is even bigger than Mercury!

Faint Rings

Unlike Saturn, Jupiter has thin, dusty rings.

Strong Magnetic Field

Jupiter’s magnetic field is 14 times stronger than Earth’s.
