Asteroids: The Space Rocks of Our Solar System

Asteroids are rocky objects orbiting the Sun, mainly found in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter.

The Asteroid Belt

A region between Mars and Jupiter filled with countless asteroids.

Ceres - The Largest Asteroid

The first asteroid discovered and the only one classified as a dwarf planet.

Vesta - The Brightest Asteroid

One of the largest asteroids, visible from Earth with the naked eye.

Apophis - A Potential Earth Threat

An asteroid that will pass very close to Earth in 2029.
