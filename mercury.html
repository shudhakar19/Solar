Mercury: The Swift Planet

The closest planet to the Sun, full of craters and extreme temperatures.

Fastest and Smallest Planet

Mercury is the smallest planet and orbits the Sun in just 88 days, making it the fastest planet in our solar system.

Rocky and Cratered

Mercury’s surface looks similar to the Moon, covered in impact craters from asteroids and comets.

Extreme Temperatures

Mercury experiences temperatures from -180°C at night to 430°C in the day due to its lack of atmosphere.

Thin Exosphere

Mercury doesn’t have a proper atmosphere, but a thin exosphere made of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, and other gases.
